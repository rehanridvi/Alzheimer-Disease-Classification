#Outcome:
The models show varying performance on the dataset, with Decision Tree  achieving an accuracy of 87.44%, indicating the best classification results. Logistic Regression follows closely with 84.03% accuracy, demonstrating strong performance for probabilistic classification. KNN, with 73,48% accuracy, performs decently but slightly lags behind the other two, possibly due to overfitting or underfitting. Overall, Decision Tree outperforms the other models, while Logistic Regression remains a strong contender, and KNN shows room for improvement. The confusion matrices can provide deeper insights into misclassifications, but accuracy-wise, Decision Tree stands as the most reliable model.
